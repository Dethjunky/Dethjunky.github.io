About

GAÏA GAÏA is a Laidback Luxury brand founded in 2020 by Quince & Zoe Karssen. Inspired by art, pop culture and women with a strong sense of independence.The brand aesthetic is casual and feminine but not without an air of masculinity. Our garments are both a reflection of freedom and true raw expression. Crafted from superior fabrics and produced in limited quantities, GAÏA GAÏA offers a conscious approach to the future of luxury casual wear.
