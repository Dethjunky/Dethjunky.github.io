DESIGNING STORIES & SHAPING BRANDS

Established by Quince and Zoe Karssen, Anti Hero is a creative agency born from a rich, ten-year journey in the fashion world, marked by successful collaborations with brands such as Zoe Karssen, Gaia Gaia, Vogue, Denham, Tommy Hilfiger, Tesento, Diesel, Adidas and many more. Anti Hero aims to make a profound impact on the cultural landscape. Blending narrative-driven designs and brand storytelling to create identities and experiences that inspire.
